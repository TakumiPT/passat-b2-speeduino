FUTURE IMPROVEMENTS - FUEL
================================================================================

⚠️ 1. DFCO (DECEL FUEL CUT) - CURRENTLY OFF
   Status: Disabled
   Benefit: Better fuel economy, engine braking
   
   RECOMMENDED SETTINGS:
   - Enable: On
   - DFCO RPM: 1500 (cut above this)
   - DFCO TPS: 2% (cut when throttle closed)
   - DFCO Hysteresis: 200 RPM
   - DFCO MAP: 30 kPa

   HOW IT WORKS:
   - When coasting (throttle closed, RPM > 1500)
   - Speeduino cuts fuel completely
   - Saves fuel, provides engine braking
   - Resumes fuel below 1500 RPM or when throttle opens

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

⚠️ 2. EGO CLOSED LOOP - CURRENTLY OFF
   Status: Wideband connected but "No correction"
   Benefit: Auto-tune AFR at cruise, better economy
   
   RECOMMENDED SETTINGS (when ready):
   - Algorithm: Simple
   - Min CLT: 70C (only when warm)
   - Min RPM: 1200
   - Max TPS: 60% (disable at WOT)
   - Authority: ±15%
   
   CAUTION: Only enable after VE table is reasonably close.
   Let it fine-tune, not fix major errors.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

⚠️ 3. AFR PROTECTION - CURRENTLY OFF
   Status: Disabled
   Benefit: Protects engine from lean condition damage
   
   RECOMMENDED SETTINGS:
   - Enable: On
   - Min MAP: 80 kPa (only at high load)
   - Min RPM: 2000
   - Max AFR: 16.0 (cut spark/fuel if leaner)
   - Cut Time: 1.0 sec
   
   HOW IT WORKS:
   - If AFR > 16.0 at high load, Speeduino cuts spark/fuel
   - Prevents melted pistons from lean detonation

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

⚠️ 4. BARO CORRECTION - CURRENTLY OFF
   Status: Disabled
   Benefit: Adjusts for altitude changes
   
   RECOMMENDED:
   - Enable: On (if MAP sensor supports it)
   - Adds/removes fuel based on atmospheric pressure
   - Useful if driving in mountains

HARDWARE NOTES

⚠️ INJECTOR RESISTOR:
   - Current: No resistor (2 ohm injector direct to Speeduino)
   - Risk: High current (7A) may damage MOSFET over time
   - RECOMMENDED: Add 3.3 ohm 25W resistor in series
   - Result: ~2.6A, safer for Speeduino output

⚠️ IAC STEPPER:
   - Issue: "Stepper Power = Always" burned out 2 steppers
   - FIX: Set "Stepper Power = Active"
   - Coils should only energize during stepping, not constantly
