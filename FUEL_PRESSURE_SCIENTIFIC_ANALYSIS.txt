PART 6: CORRECT INTERPRETATION - VE TABLE IS THE KEY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

The VE table value of 19-20% is NOT actual volumetric efficiency.
It's a MULTIPLIER on the base fuel calculation.

In Speeduino:
  Pulsewidth = Req_Fuel × (VE / 100) × (MAP / 100) × other_corrections

At idle:
  1.199 ms = 4.2 ms × (20 / 100) × (42 / 100) × corrections
  1.199 = 4.2 × 0.20 × 0.42 × corrections  
  1.199 = 0.353 × corrections
  corrections = 3.40

This 3.40 factor includes:
- Air temperature correction (~1.0)
- Battery voltage correction (~1.0)  
- Warmup enrichment (~1.0 at 97°C)
- AFR correction (14.7 / 14.0 = 1.05)
- Other factors

So the math approximately works out.

PART 7: FUEL PRESSURE EFFECT ON FLOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Injector flow scales with SQUARE ROOT of pressure:

Flow = Rated_Flow × √(Actual_Pressure / Rated_Pressure)

IF injector is rated 60 lb/hr @ 3.0 bar:
  @ 1.5 bar: Flow = 60 × √(1.5/3.0) = 60 × 0.707 = 42.4 lb/hr
  @ 1.0 bar: Flow = 60 × √(1.0/3.0) = 60 × 0.577 = 34.6 lb/hr

IF injector is rated 60 lb/hr @ 1.0 bar:
  @ 1.5 bar: Flow = 60 × √(1.5/1.0) = 60 × 1.225 = 73.5 lb/hr
  @ 1.0 bar: Flow = 60 lb/hr (matches rating)
