INJECTOR SAFETY WARNING
================================================================================

Your setup: 2 ohm injector direct to Speeduino (no resistor)
Current: 14V / 2 ohm = 7A
Opening Time: 1.0ms (working)

RISK: 7A exceeds Speeduino MOSFET rating (~3-4A continuous)

RECOMMENDATION: Add 3.3 ohm resistor in series
- Total resistance: 5.3 ohm
- Current: 2.6A (safe for MOSFET)
- Injector will still open (tested: 7 ohm = 1.55A did NOT work)

If you keep running without resistor:
- Monitor Speeduino board temperature near INJ1
- Risk increases at sustained WOT (high duty cycle)
- MOSFET may fail after months/years
