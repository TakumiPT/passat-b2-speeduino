## How to Apply

1. **VE Table:** Fuel > VE Table → Click top-left (100kPa/500RPM) → Paste
2. **AFR Table:** Fuel > AFR Target → Click top-left → Paste
3. **AE Settings:** Fuel > Acceleration Enrichment → Enter values manually
4. **Burn to ECU**
5. **Test drive and datalog**
