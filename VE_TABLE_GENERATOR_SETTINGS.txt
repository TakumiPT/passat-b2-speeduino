WHAT IS THE VE TABLE GENERATOR?
--------------------------------
The Table Generator is a TunerStudio tool that creates a baseline VE table
based on your engine's physical characteristics. It calculates approximate
VE values at different RPM/load points based on engine physics.

This gives you a MUCH BETTER starting point than the current lean values!

WHY THIS WORKS:
================================================================================

PHYSICS-BASED CALCULATION:
--------------------------
The generator uses your engine specs to calculate:

1. Volumetric Efficiency at idle (low VE, restricted airflow)
2. VE at peak torque (highest VE, optimal breathing)
3. VE at peak power (slightly lower VE, high RPM pumping losses)
4. Interpolates all other points based on these anchors

REPLACES GUESSWORK:
-------------------
Current table: Someone guessed values, possibly corrupted by autotune
Generated table: Calculated from first principles physics

YOUR SPECIFIC ENGINE:
---------------------
1.6L DT is a simple, well-understood design:
- Moderate compression (9.0:1)
- Two valves per cylinder
- Conservative cam timing
- Should have VE of 75-85% at peak torque
- Generator will calculate this correctly!
